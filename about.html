About Me

I am a data scientist working for Uvionics Tech India Pvt Ltd. I have 3 years of experience in developing & deploying ML related applications.

I hold a Master's degree in Computational Engineering & Networking from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham university. During my masters, I have done works in image processing and natural language processing which was later converted into 3 research publications. For image processing, I worked on the performance evaluation of different filters for CBIR which was advised by Dr. Sowmya V (paper). My mini project was in NLP, parallel corpus creation which was advised by Dr Anand Kumar (paper). My thesis was on the implementation of deep learning in regional language(Malayalam) using LSTM. The knowledge base so obtained was then used to make a spellchecker which can detect character wise errors in a word. I was guided by Dr Soman K.P (paper).
