Dr. AI

Health related chatbot. Data is collected via web scraping from sites like mayoclinic, askdoctor.com. Inputs are passed via text as well as speech input. Here a custom NLU unit is used for classifying the sentence and then extracting the entity(words) from them. This unit is then integrated to a database for reply generation back to the user.
